Sponsors

Zaks.com


Zaks.com has graciously been providing our mailing list service since our group first started and provides Web site development and hosting.

New Horizons Computer Learning Center

New Horizons Computer Learning Center provides a wide range of technical, application, and business skills courses taught by certified instructors and has been providing us with a location to host our meetings for the past few years.

Wellington Steele & Associates


Wellington Steele & Associates is a privately held, locally owned, recruiting and outsourcing company founded in 2002 and headquartered in Rochester, NY and regularly sponsors the food before our monthly meetings.

Microsoft Technical Communities


Offers registered user groups and speakers benefits such as moeny for event promotions, discount vouchers, and SWAG.

ineta.org


We have been a registered member of ineta.org since it's founding, leveraging the community to find and fund speakers from outside the Rochester region to present on topics of interest to our members.

O'Reilly


The O'Reilly user group program offers a consistant 40% discount on print and 50% discount on eBook purchases as well as discounts for technical conferences and events. In addition, we occasionally receive preview copies of books for writting an honest review.
