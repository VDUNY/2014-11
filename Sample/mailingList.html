Mailing List

Email List Server Information

To join the email list, send an email (from the account you wish to join from) to listserv@zaks.com and type only the words subscribe vduny {Your Name} in the body of the email. (ex: subscribe vduny Joe Smith)

To get off the email list, you need to send an email to listserv@zaks.com and type only the words unsubscribe vduny in the body of the email. You must send the email from the same account that you wish to be removed from the list. If you no longer have this account, contact Kyle Korndoerfer or Joel Bennett and request thatthey manually delete your name.

Purpose

The purpose of the email list is mainly to receive announcements about when and where the meetings are, as well as giving the members of the list an opportunity to email questions or announcements to the whole group when they are unable to attend the meeting in person.

Protocol

We have all enjoyed the fact that vduny is a low-traffic list. Please remember to stay on topic. Send visual development questions or announcements (jobs/hiring/upcoming seminars/etc...) that are appropriate to present to the whole group by emailing vduny@zaks.com.

There's no "Undo"! Whatever you send to vduny@zaks.com gets automatically posted to all the people on the list!

Replies

Avoid replying to vduny@zaks.com, unless everyone needs to know. Once conversation starts, it is best to exchange email addresses and continue the email thread directly with the person who initiated the question.

To ask questions of, or to make announcements to the group, address the email to vduny@zaks.com. To ask a question about the group or volunteer for a presentation or to sponsor a meeting, contact either Kyle Korndoerfer (kyle@vduny.org) or Joel Bennett (jaykul@huddledmasses.org).
